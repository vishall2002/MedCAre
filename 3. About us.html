MedCare Hospital

Medcare is a Private Hospital setup in 2016 and is situated in V V Puram, Bengaluru city center. The Hospital has a capacity of 30 beds with dedicated 8 Intensive / critical care beds, 3 ventilators, 3 BIPAPs , 3 private rooms, 6 semi private rooms, 8 economy rooms (general wards). Our 2 sophisticated operation theaters and 3 specific dedicated out-patient consultation rooms, and 2 emergency / treatment room complete the infrastructure of the hospital to offer the highest levels of medical care to our patients.

Medcare hospital provides wide array of services with most medical, surgical specialties as well as Digital X Ray facilities, physiotherapy, laboratory investigations and pharmacy available 24 /7.

  • The Hospital's highly specialized patient care adheres to the highest standards in serving patients from all walks of life through multi-disciplinary approach to treatment.
  • We maintain state-of-the-art facilities and best medical expertise to ensure a high level, integrated tertiary service and ICUs with round the clock monitoring.
  • We work hard to protect the hospital reputation for excellence and enjoy good relationships with patients.
  • The focus of the hospital is to take care of all patients in a caring, comfortable and safe environment, while maintaining the much needed privacy.
  • With this hospital, we continue to keep up with its commitment towards bringing quality healthcare closer and accessible to all.
  • There are ongoing initiatives in place for enhancing patient safety, patient satisfaction and clinical excellence and quality care to provide the most comprehensive, advanced and compassionate care to the patient and family.


Medcare hospital is one of the few centres in Bengaluru city which provides comprehensive Burns Care.

Burns care service is managed by a multidisciplinary committed team of experts comprising of senior experienced plastic surgeons, intensivist, general physician , emergency physician, psychiatrist, physiotherapist, dietician and nurses with special training in caring for burns patients.

The seven specialities that we would focus on are Plastic & reconstructive surgery, Burns care, laproscopic and minimal access surgery, vascular surgery, Trauma and Orthopedics surgery and oncosurgeries led by a team of experts with combined experience of over 100yrs.

MEDCARE Hospital also provides comprehensive care for complex critically injured trauma patients and for wounds resulting from trauma, diabetes or vascular disease that are difficult or slow to heal. In addition the hospital caters general surgery patients, cerebrovascular patients requiring blood clot therapy and patients requiring intensive care observation.

Medcare hospital is recognized by all private health insurers and remain competitively and fairly priced for those without health insurance.
